Sea Otters!

  1. First Fact
    • Sea otters are the smallest marine mammals in the world.
    • Adult males weigh between 64 and 88 pounds (29-40 kg) and adult females weigh between 37 and 64 pounds (17-29 kg).
  2. Second Fact
    • Sea otters have the densest fur of any mammal, with up to 1 million hairs per square inch.
    • They rely on their thick fur to keep them warm in cold ocean waters.
  3. Third Fact
    • Sea otters are known for using rocks as tools to crack open the shells of their prey, which includes clams, mussels, and other shellfish.
    • They also use their chests as a hard surface to smash open shells.
