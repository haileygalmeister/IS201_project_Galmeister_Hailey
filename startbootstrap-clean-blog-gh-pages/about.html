About Me

Professional Life

As an aspiring event planner, I am dedicated to designing exceptional experiences that enhance enjoyment and better the lives of others. With a passion for connecting with both individuals and groups, I strive to create events that make a lasting impact and leave a positive impression on all attendees.

I am excited to begin an internship in the near future at the St. Regis in Deer Valley, where I will further develop my skills in event planning and management.

I am a hardworking and dedicated individual who is committed to making a positive impact in the lives of others through well-organized events and experiences. I am open to any opportunity to help with events, and I am confident in my ability to contribute to any team.

Personal Life

I am from a small city in Southern California called Dana Point. I grew up playing tennis, swimming in the ocean, and flying planes. I love to travel, and my favorite places I have been are Grindelwald, Switzerland, and Queenstown, New Zealand.
